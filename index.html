ABOUT US

We have been operating on the Czech market for over 12 years. We are currently among the leading specialists in complete high-current and low-current electrical installations. We install distribution systems in apartments, family houses and industrial halls. The advantage of working with us is not only money saved - we don't charge anything extra and our prices are very friendly all year round, but also time saved. Our services are comprehensive. You don't need to hire experts from different companies. In addition to professional high-current and low-current electrical installation, we also provide lightning rod installation and provide professional inspection reports. If it is within our means, we will be happy to accommodate you in everything.
